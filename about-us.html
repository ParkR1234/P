FAQ

If you need a little more information.

Why have DVLA passed on the details of the Registered owner?

As a member of the BPA's Approved Operator Scheme, Park Right Services have the ability to obtain the information from the DVLA


When will I hear back from my appeal?

Park Right Services aim to respond to appeals as soon as possible, this can take up to one month. Rest assured, your ticket charge value will remain the same until dealt with.


I wasn't driving, do I still have to pay?

Park Right Services follows procedure from the BPA code of practice, where if the registered keeper can report whom the driver at that period was, funds may be recovered from the driver. If funds are unrecovable from the driver for 28 days, the registered keeper is then liable.


What happens if I ignore the charge?

Park Right Services will review all parking charges after 28 days and may take further action such as Credit Reference Agencies, Debt Recovery or Court Proceedings, which will incur additional cost.
